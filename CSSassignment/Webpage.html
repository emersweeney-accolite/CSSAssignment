Pastel Purple

Pastel purple is a soft, pale shade of purple with the hex code #C3B1E1, a color with high value and low saturation in the HSV color space. Pastels were frequently used during and are often associated with the Impressionist and Art Deco eras.

Periwinkle

Periwinkle is a pale, violet-blue color with the hex code #CCCCFF, also frequently referred to as pastel blue. Periwinkle, named after the flower of the same name, is a shade within the indigo family--sitting between blue and violet on the color wheel.
